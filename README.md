# Blinkit-Analysis-Dashboard
## 1.	Project Title:
⚡ Blinkit Insights: Quick-Commerce Performance & Operations Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze delivery operations, customer behavior, inventory management, marketing performance, and overall business efficiency for a quick-commerce platform.

## 2.	Description:
The Blinkit Analysis Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI solution developed to monitor key operational and business metrics of a quick-commerce platform. It provides deep insights into order trends, delivery performance, customer retention, inventory status, and revenue generation.

This dashboard helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions by visualizing complex datasets in a simple and interactive way, improving operational efficiency and enhancing customer experience.

## 3.	Tech Stack:
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 Power BI Desktop – Data visualization and dashboard creation

• 📂 Power Query – Data transformation and preprocessing

• 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – KPIs, measures, and calculated fields

• 🔗 Data Modeling – Relationships between orders, customers, inventory, and regions

• 🗄️ SQL – Data extraction and structured querying

## 4.	Data Source:
### Source: Blinkit Quick-Commerce Operational Database

• Order Data (order ID, date, delivery status, delivery time)

• Customer Data (customer ID, retention, feedback)

• Revenue Data (order value, profit margins)

• Inventory Data (stock levels, product categories)

• Marketing Data (campaign ROI, acquisition channels)

• Regional Data (city/region-wise performance)

## 5.	Features / Highlights: 
### 🔎 Business Problem

Quick-commerce platforms generate massive real-time data across orders, inventory, logistics, and customer interactions. Without a centralized dashboard:

• Tracking delivery performance and delays becomes difficult.

• Inventory mismanagement can lead to stockouts or overstocking.

• Customer retention and satisfaction trends are unclear.

• Marketing ROI and acquisition channels are hard to evaluate.

• Revenue insights across regions and categories are fragmented.

### 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

To build an interactive analytics dashboard that:

• Tracks order and delivery trends over time.

• Monitors delivery success and operational efficiency.

• Analyzes customer retention and feedback sentiment.

• Evaluates inventory levels and stock performance.

• Measures marketing campaign effectiveness.

• Provides revenue insights across regions and product categories.

### 📊 Key Dashboard Insights

#### 📦 Operations Overview

• Total deliveries trend (daily/monthly performance)

• Delivery completion rate (success vs failed deliveries)

• Regional revenue contribution

#### 👥 Customer Insights

• Customer retention rate

• Top customers by revenue

• Customer feedback & sentiment analysis

#### 📢 Marketing Analytics

• Campaign ROI comparison

• Customer acquisition channels

• Marketing spend by region

#### 📦 Inventory Management

• Stock levels by product category

• Inventory turnover ratio

• Low stock alerts for restocking

#### 💰 Sales Performance

• Revenue by product category

• Monthly sales trends

• Average Order Value (AOV)
